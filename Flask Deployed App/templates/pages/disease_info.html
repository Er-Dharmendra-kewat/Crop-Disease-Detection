Plant Diseases information and medication

Plant Disease Prevention Method Medicine Identification Technique
Powdery Mildew Ensure good air circulation, remove infected plants. Neem oil, Sulfur-based fungicides. White powdery spots on leaves.
Rust Remove infected plant parts, plant resistant varieties. Copper fungicides, Mancozeb. Orange, yellow, or brown pustules on leaves.
Blight Avoid overhead watering, remove infected plants. Chlorothalonil, Copper-based fungicides. Dark lesions on leaves, stems, and fruit.
Downy Mildew Ensure proper drainage, remove infected debris. Fungicides containing metalaxyl, Mancozeb. Yellowing of leaves with white mold on the underside.
Leaf Spot Space plants properly to improve air circulation. Neem oil, Copper fungicides. Small, dark spots on the leaves.
Late Blight Water plants at the base, remove infected leaves. Metalaxyl, Copper-based fungicides. Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems.
Anthracnose Prune affected areas, improve spacing of plants. Fungicides like Chlorothalonil and Mancozeb. Dark, sunken lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits.
Fusarium Wilt Practice crop rotation, improve soil drainage. No specific medicine; soil sterilization helps. Yellowing of leaves, wilting of plant.
Verticillium Wilt Plant resistant varieties, improve soil structure. No specific medicine; improve soil management. Wilting, yellowing of leaves, stunted growth.
Tomato Mosaic Virus Remove infected plants, use virus-free seeds. No medicine; removal of infected plants. Yellowing and mottling of leaves, stunted growth.
Leaf Curl Remove infected branches, control aphids. Insecticides for aphid control. Curled, deformed leaves.
Powdery Scab Rotate crops, avoid planting in wet conditions. Fungicides containing Mancozeb. White powdery coating on tubers.
Clubroot Ensure soil drainage, avoid overwatering. Fungicides like Lime and Sulfur treatments. Swelling of roots, stunted growth.
Yellow Rust Plant resistant varieties, remove infected plants. Fungicides like Triadimefon. Yellow pustules on leaves.
Tomato Blight Water plants at the base, remove infected parts. Metalaxyl, Chlorothalonil. Dark, watery lesions on leaves and fruits.
Black Rot Use resistant varieties, crop rotation. Chlorothalonil, Copper fungicides. Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves.
Leaf Curl Control aphids, prune infected branches. Insecticides for aphids. Curled, distorted leaves.
Apple Scab Prune for air circulation, plant resistant varieties. Captan, Sulfur fungicides. Olive-colored spots on leaves and fruits.
Phytophthora Root Rot Improve drainage, avoid overwatering. Metalaxyl, Phosphorous acid fungicides. Yellowing, wilting of leaves, root decay.
Leaf Blight Practice crop rotation, water at the base of plants. Fungicides like Mancozeb, Chlorothalonil. Yellow lesions on leaves.
Gray Mold Avoid overhead watering, improve airflow. Fungicides with Boscalid, Pyraclostrobin. Grayish fuzzy mold on flowers and leaves.
Botrytis Blight Remove infected flowers, ensure good air circulation. Botrycide, Captan fungicides. Gray mold on flowers and leaves.
Soft Rot Maintain clean plant surroundings, improve drainage. Antibacterial treatments like Oxytetracycline. Soft, mushy spots on plant tissues.
Brown Rot Remove infected fruit, improve airflow. Captan, Thiabendazole fungicides. Rotting, brown lesions on fruit.
Cherry Leaf Spot Remove fallen leaves, prune infected branches. Chlorothalonil, Mancozeb fungicides. Red or purple spots on leaves.
Grape Downy Mildew Remove affected leaves, control humidity. Mancozeb, Copper fungicides. Yellowing and white mold on leaves.
Chili Wilt Rotate crops, use disease-free seeds. No specific medicine, remove infected plants. Wilting, yellowing of leaves.
Peach Leaf Curl Prune affected branches, use resistant varieties. Chlorothalonil, Copper-based fungicides. Reddened, curled leaves on peaches.
Blackleg Use disease-resistant seeds, crop rotation. Seed treatment with fungicides like Apron. Blackening of stems at the soil line.
Anthracnose on Mango Prune infected branches, improve drainage. Chlorothalonil, Copper-based fungicides. Dark, sunken lesions on mango fruits and leaves.
Phomopsis Blight Remove infected plant parts, improve air circulation. Chlorothalonil, Mancozeb. Dark, sunken lesions on leaves and stems.
Wheat Yellow Rust Use resistant varieties, avoid wet conditions. Triadimefon fungicides. Yellow-orange pustules on leaves.
Carrot Root Rot Avoid overwatering, use good soil drainage. Fungicides with Thiabendazole. Soft, decaying roots.
Botrytis Canker Prune affected branches, improve drainage. Fungicides like Captan, Chlorothalonil. Brown cankers on stems and twigs.
Fusarium Crown Rot Improve soil drainage, avoid soil compaction. No specific medicine; improve soil management. Wilting, yellowing of leaves, crown rot.
Red Thread Keep lawn mowed, water appropriately. Fungicides like Mancozeb. Red, thread-like fungal growth on grass blades.
Leaf Spot on Peach Remove infected leaves, prune trees. Chlorothalonil, Mancozeb. Dark spots with yellow halos on peach leaves.
Soilborne Fungi Improve soil drainage, crop rotation. Soil fumigation, biological control agents. Wilting, yellowing of leaves.
Powdery Mildew on Grapes Increase airflow, prune infected parts. Sulfur-based fungicides. White, powdery fungal growth on leaves and grapes.
